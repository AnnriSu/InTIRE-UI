Check Tire Pressure

Always maintain the recommended tire pressure to prevent uneven wear and improve fuel efficiency.

Rotate Tires Regularly

Rotate your tires every 5,000–8,000 km to ensure even tread wear and extend tire lifespan.

Inspect for Cracks or Cuts

Look for sidewall cracks, punctures, or objects stuck in the tread that may cause damage.

Monitor Tire Age

Tires should generally be replaced after 5–6 years even if the tread still looks good.
